Our Story

Bringing Costumes
to Life in Pune

A family-run creative rental studio dedicated to making school events, stage performances, and celebrations truly unforgettable.

Welcome to Shraswa Paridhan Drapery & Rental Studio — a creative rental service born from a simple belief: every child deserves to shine on stage.

Based in Baner, Pune, we specialize in costumes and drapery for school events, stage performances, and celebrations. Our collection spans animals, mythology, cultural characters, historical roles, and fancy dress — all maintained to the highest standards of quality and hygiene.

What We Offer

  • Animal costumes — lion, tiger, peacock, rabbit, elephant & more
  • Mythological characters — Krishna, Rama, Sita, Hanuman, Durga & more
  • Freedom fighters and historical role costumes
  • Fancy dress competition outfits for children of all ages
  • Stage drapery and school event decoration materials

Our Mission

To support schools, teachers, and parents by providing affordable, creative, and high-quality costumes that bring learning and performances to life. We believe that the right costume sparks imagination, builds confidence, and creates memories that last a lifetime.

Why Schools Trust Us

  • Widest collection of school event costumes in Baner, Pune
  • Cleaned and well-maintained outfits — safe for every child
  • Affordable rental pricing designed for school budgets
  • Timely delivery — always ready before your event
  • Easy booking for bulk school orders via WhatsApp

Our Promise

We are committed to making school events more colorful, educational, and memorable. Every costume we rent is a story waiting to be told. Let's make every school performance worth remembering! 🎉
